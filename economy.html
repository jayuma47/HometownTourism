ECONOMY


Kyoto's economy is a mixture of the old and new. The city is one of the most popular destinations in Japan, it attracts many visitors each year. The city's stunning temples, scenic gardens, and traditional festivals contributes the local economy. Travel businesses like hotels, restaurants and shops thrive thanks to visitors.




Kyoto's traditional crafts such us making kimono, ceramics, and lacquerware are often made by skilled artisan. They keep the old ways preserves which add to this city's charm and economy. Aside from traditions, Kyoto's tech industry has grown in recent years mostly from electronics, robotics, and biotechnology. The city is also home of the largest entertainment company Nintendo.
