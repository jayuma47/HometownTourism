ABOUT KYOTO


Kyoto is the former capital city of Japan for over 1,000 years, is rich in history and culture. This city is known for its temples, shrines, and traditional houses, which showcases the Japanese architecture. Today, it becomes one the major business district in Japan and recommended for anyone who wants to visit.




The city of Kyoto is dedicated to preserve its cultural traditions which date back to the Heian period. You can enjoy calm gardens, tea ceremonies, and its famous performance of geisha culture.
